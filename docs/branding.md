# B.I.C. Branding Notes

## Color Palette
- Primary Red: #EC0C0C (accent from swoosh)
- Dark Graphite: #252525 (background)
- Charcoal: #1B1B1B (hero overlay / footer)
- Soft Gray: #F5F5F7 (section background)
- Pure White: #FFFFFF (typography)

## Typography
- Headings: "Poppins" bold, uppercase tracking similar to Rentic hero titles.
- Body: "Inter" regular for optimal legibility across devices.
- Numerals: Use tabular numbers for pricing blocks.

## Imagery & Styling
- Use high-contrast imagery with dark vignette overlays so red CTAs pop.
- Buttons: pill-shaped with slight inner shadow, hover transitions to brighter red (#FF3535).
- Icons: outline style, monochrome white on dark, charcoal on light backgrounds.

## Language Tone
- Confident, expert, focused on secure parallel import.
- Highlight 25-country availability, CB RF exchange rate, and compliance.
